### Introduction

At Al-Muta'allim Application, we are fully committed to protecting our users' privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with Egyptian Personal Data Protection Law No. 151 of 2020, and international best practices for student data protection.

---

### 1. Data We Collect

We collect only data necessary to provide our educational services:

- **Full Name:** For digital identity creation and personalization
- **Email Address:** For authentication and official communication
- **Phone Number:** For emergency contact and verification
- **Date of Birth:** For age verification and personalization
- **Gender:** For anonymous statistical analysis
- **Grade Level:** For customizing educational content
- **Governorate and Area:** For matching with appropriate teachers
- **Parent/Guardian Phone:** For emergency communication

**Important Note:** We do not collect precise geolocation data, nor do we request personal photos except in specific cases with explicit consent.

---

### 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

- Personalizing learning experience based on grade level
- Enabling secure communication between students and teachers
- Creating educational progress reports for students and parents
- Sending essential notifications only (educational and security)
- Anonymous statistical analysis to improve service
- Congratulating users on personal occasions (optional)

**What We Don't Do:**

- We do not sell your data to any third party
- We do not use your data for targeted advertising
- We do not share your data with commercial entities

---

### 3. Children's Data Protection

Given our target age group (12-17 years), we implement strict standards:

**For Students Under 16:**

- We obtain verified parental consent before account creation
- Parents can access all student data and activities
- Parents have the right to request data deletion at any time
- We don't allow data sharing with teachers without parental consent

**Consent Mechanism:**

- Sending verification link to parent's email
- Or SMS verification code to parent's phone
- Electronic signature on consent form

---

### 4. Data Sharing

We only share data in the following cases:

**With Verified Teachers:** We share name, grade, and academic progress for teaching and monitoring

**With Technical Service Providers:** Encrypted data only for data hosting and security

**With Legal Authorities:** As per court order for legal compliance

**We Do Not Share Data With:** Advertising companies, social media platforms, or non-educational entities

---

### 5. Security Measures

We use bank-level protection technologies:

- **Encryption:** AES-256 for storage and TLS 1.3 for transmission
- **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) system
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 intrusion detection systems
- **Backup:** Encrypted backups in multiple locations
- **Testing:** Regular penetration testing by certified security firms

---

### 6. Your Rights

Under Egyptian law, you have the right to:

1. **Right of Access:** Request a copy of all your data
2. **Right to Rectification:** Correct any inaccurate information
3. **Right to Erasure:** Request permanent deletion of your account and data
4. **Right to Object:** Refuse use of your data for analytical purposes
5. **Right to Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format

**Exercising Rights:**

- Via account settings within the app
- Or via email: privacy@almutaallim.app
- Response time: Maximum 30 days

---

### 7. Data Retention

- **Active Data:** Throughout your use of the application
- **After Account Cancellation:** Immediate deletion within 30 days
- **Legal Records:** We may retain some data for 5 years as per legal requirements

---

### 8. Updates

We will notify you of any substantial changes via:

- In-app notification
- Email for important changes
- 30 days before implementing major changes
